Quick reference

FTE & Hours Calculator

Target FTE is the single most consequential field on a position. Convert it to Hours Per Week — or work backwards from hours to FTE — without doing the arithmetic by hand. Figures here are net hours, excluding ADO accrual.

Quick fill:

Austin Health's standard is 38 hours, net of ADO accrual — don't add ADO hours on top when entering this figure in SuccessFactors.
